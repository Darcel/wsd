About Us

Mission Statement

At Highway to Heaven we believe in the infallibility of the Bible, and it is founded on faith in Jesus Christ, the Son of God. The purpose of the church is to teach the Gospel message of salvation, to nurture the believer, to preserve sound doctrine and to serve others in the likeness of Christ.

Vision Statement

Our vision is to have a church where souls may come and be delivered from the clutches of Satan and be enriched and empowered by the word of God that they too may become Disciples of Christ. To reach out to a dying society and to compelle men to come into the fellowship of Christ as the scripture states it in Luke 14:23 "And the Lord said unto the servant, Go out into the highways and hedges, and compel them to come in, that my house may be filled", for we know that Satan will not let them go, but with the authority of God's word, love, mercy and grace it shall be so. To be a community leader by way of prison ministry, missionary ministry, and evangelizing. The scripture state that we should be the head and not the tail. A lot may think this scripture means MONEY ONLY but, I not only believe that it mean in our finances but also in society for we are the light of the world.
